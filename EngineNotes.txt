Stats:
[0] = Health Points
[1] = Magic Points
[2] = Attack
[3] = Magic
[4] = Speed
[5] = Luck
[6] = Defence
[7] = Resistance

Positions:
0 - 2 = Party Members (3 Party Members)
3 - 6 = Enemies (No more than 4 enemies)
There's probably some better way to do this

Skill/Item Types:
0 = Physical Damage
1 = Magic Damage
2 = HP Restore
3 = MP Restore
4 = Stat Buff
5 = Stat Debuff
6 = Status Effect
